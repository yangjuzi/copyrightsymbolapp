⚡ Auto-Correction Method

Set Up Text Replacement

  1. Open Settings app
  2. Tap General
  3. Tap Keyboard
  4. Tap Text Replacement
  5. Tap the + button in top-right
  6. In Phrase, type: ©
  7. In Shortcut, type: (c)
  8. Tap Save

Pro tip: Now whenever you type "(c)", it will automatically change to ©

🎤 Dictation Method

Use Siri Dictation

  1. Tap in a text field
  2. Tap the microphone icon 🎤 on the keyboard
  3. Say clearly: "copyright symbol"
  4. The © symbol will appear

Works with: "copyright sign", "copyright symbol", "copy symbol"
